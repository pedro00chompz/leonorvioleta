AGENDA PARALELO

AGORA PORTO

TEATRO MUNICIPAL DO PORTO

2020

For IPO Coimbra (Portuguese Institute of Oncology), Balance is the union between happiness and serenity. Values that are transmitted by a dynamic scenario of harmonious colors, created with elements alluding to nature, the place that often makes us reach fullness. An abstract landscape, a mix of sensations and directions that seek balance, as required by those who pass by: find a balance, a comfort, a new look at the landscape.
